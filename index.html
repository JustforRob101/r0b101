About Us

Our story (How it came to be)

We started this company to give back to our community and give affordable and quality services compared to other high end service providers.

We at Caltech Appliances LLC are a family owned business. We understand the importance of appliances and their everyday uses. We understand the types of emergencies that come up.  Let Caltech Appliances take care of your problems; we guarantee you're in good hands. Caltech provides an affordable service that is unmatched by big name appliance repair companies. With over 20 years of experience in equipment and repair, we use our knowledge, training, and expertise to provide reliable and honest service. We specialize in refrigeration, but we also work on washers, dryers, dishwashers, cooktops, and free standing ice makers. Contact Caltech Appliances LLC for a free consultation at 512-919-2047.
